# Skill: CNESST Dataset Structure
## Source: https://www.donneesquebec.ca/recherche/dataset/lesions-professionnelles
- 6 CSV files, 697,602 records (2018-2023)
- 13 columns: ID, NATURE_LESION, SIEGE_LESION, GENRE, AGENT_CAUSAL_LESION, SEXE_PERS_PHYS, GROUPE_AGE, SECTEUR_SCIAN, IND_LESION_SURDITE/MACHINE/TMS/PSY/COVID_19
- 22 SCIAN sectors, NO geographic data in CSV
- 2022 anomaly: 161,962 records (COVID surge in Sante & Social: 74,517)
